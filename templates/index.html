Project Hexsight

The six-team build exercise — six coordinated roles, one working Security Operations Center in a single day. Five teams built SOC components; the sixth team (the Inspector) audited the build against a declared protocol and produced bilingual take-home training materials on how AI was used. Six sets of eyes, one field of view — hence the name. Team assignments, repos, and the shared protocol are on the Day 3 exercise page; SSH credentials are on the access cards page.
